Hi! I am Rui, a UX/UI designer with multi-cultural study experience 🗺️

Me Sketchbook Travel Diary Gardening

I am a UX/UI designer @ TikTok.

I always like to ask why. Instead of jumping to devise an idea,
I like to first know if the problem exists and what is the truth behind.

I believe that the power of design in technology is to build a communication bridge between human beings and machines. As a user experience designer, I aim to dig out how the user is behaving, what the user is thinking, and what the user needs.
I also believe in the importance of better understanding the user’s mental model and better designing an interface to fit what the users expects...and beyond!

Let's get in touch! I would love to exchange ideas with you about my work, my experience, and my design thinking.
